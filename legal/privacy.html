Plain-English Summary: I collect minimal data — your email if you sign up for something, basic site analytics. I don't sell your data. You can ask me to delete it anytime. GDPR and CCPA rights are respected.

iamjax.me is my personal website — Jax Scott (Jaclyn Scott), AI educator, cybersecurity expert, and founder of Outpost Gray. This Privacy Policy explains how I collect and use your information when you visit this site. Privacy questions: jax@outpostgray.com

2b. What's Collected Automatically

4. I Don't Sell Your Data

I do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone. Full stop.

5. Cookies

My site uses cookies for basic analytics and functionality. I don't use cookies to track you across other websites or serve targeted ads. You can control cookies in your browser settings.

I keep your information only as long as needed to serve you and comply with legal requirements. Request deletion anytime — I'll process it within 30 days.

You have rights to access, rectify, erase, restrict, and port your data. You may also withdraw consent and file complaints with your supervisory authority. Contact: jax@outpostgray.com

10. CCPA — California Residents

For California residents: You have the right to know what data I collect about you, the right to delete it, and the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. I do not sell personal information under CCPA. Contact: jax@outpostgray.com

This site is not intended for children under 13. I do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If I learn I've collected such information, I'll delete it promptly.

Refund Policy

Quick Summary

  • Digital downloads: No refunds — all sales final

No Refunds

Digital Downloads

All digital product sales are final and non-refundable. This includes PDF guides, e-books, templates, checklists, swipe files, and any other downloadable digital content.

Because digital files are delivered immediately upon purchase and can't be "returned," I can't offer refunds on these items. This is industry-standard for digital goods.

Please read product descriptions carefully before buying. If you have questions about what's included, email me first: jax@outpostgray.com

7-Day Window

Online Courses

I stand behind my course content. If you're not satisfied, you can request a refund within 7 calendar days of purchase, provided you've accessed less than 20% of the course content.

Both conditions must be met. After 7 days or after 20% completion — whichever comes first — refunds will not be issued.

To request a refund: email jax@outpostgray.com with subject "Course Refund Request" within the 7-day window. Include your name and purchase email. I'll process eligible refunds within 5–10 business days.

Separate Agreement

Consulting Services

Professional consulting through Outpost Gray is governed by a separate written service agreement. Refund and cancellation terms are defined in that agreement, not this policy.

Questions about consulting billing: jax@outpostgray.com

Got a Billing Problem?

If something went wrong — a technical glitch, a billing error, or you received something materially different from what was described — please contact me before filing a chargeback. I'm a real person and I want to make it right.

Email: jax@outpostgray.com — subject "Billing Issue." I'll respond within 2 business days.
